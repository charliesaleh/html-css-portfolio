About Me

Who I am

Good day, my name is Charlie Saleh. I was born on May 10, 2001 in Dearborn, Michigan. I am currently pursuing a career as a software engineer. I'm attending Henry Ford College with the intention of transferring to either Wayne State University or Eastern Michigan University. In order to study and further my passion in coding, I am now enrolled in Coding Temple's bootcamp, which begins on January 30th, 2023.

My Interests

Reading, Watching, Cooking, and Hanging out with my friends.

My interests include reading books like as shonen manga, manwhas, and light novels, watching shows on Netflix, Crunchyroll, or HBO MAX, making various meals that I enjoy, and hanging out or playing video games with my friends whenever possible. My two favorite series are Attack on Titan on Crunchyroll and The Last Kingdom on Netflix. Solo Leveling is my favorite tale to read (which will soon be adapted into an anime) since it has a terrific premise and is nicely designed. Steaks served medium rare are without a doubt my favorite dish of all time. I hang out with my friends whenever I have the chance, and there is never a dull day when we get together.

My Goals for the Future

To transform my life and the lives of my family members.

I've been living in a one bedroom and one bedroom apartment with a family of four since I was a child, and I've seen my parents suffer throughout their lives paying expenses just so my sister and I could have a wonderful life, for which I will be eternally thankful. It's now my turn to reciprocate their generosity. My ambition is to become a professional software engineer in order to financially support myself and my family. I want to buy a property for myself and my parents so they may move about freely and comfortably. These are goals I want to achieve, but I'm going to start with Coding Temple so I can get that done before moving on to other things.
